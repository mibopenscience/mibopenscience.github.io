Mission

Ensure that all research conducted at XXX is of high quality, reproducible, transparant and efficient

Approach

We offer three services to support our mission: a helpdesk advising on (quantative and qualitative) methods, stats, FAIR data and open science, a toolbox with open science tools and procedures, and a training program

Helpdesk

Need advice on qualitative or quantative methods, stats or FAIR data management? Please contact the helpdesk

Toolbox

Curious how to practice open science? Please consult our open science manual

Training

Workshops and events, and a curated list of external training possibilities

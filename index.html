MiB Open Science support

[NOTE: this is a temporary pilot website]

Mission

Ensure that all research conducted in the program Mensen in Beweging is of high quality, reproducible, transparant and efficient

Approach

We offer three services to support our mission: advice on (quantative and qualitative) methods, stats, FAIR data and open science, a toolbox with open science tools and procedures, and a training program

Advice

Need advice on qualitative or quantative methods, stats or FAIR data management? Please contact the Support desk

Toolbox

Curious how to practice open science? Please consult our open science manual

Training

Workshops and events, and a curated list of external training possibilities

FAIR data

FAIR data is about findable, accessible, interoperable and reusable data. In the Urban Vitality seed money project 'FAIR: geen woorden maar data' a multidisciplinary team centred around data stewards explores how to implement the FAIR data principles.

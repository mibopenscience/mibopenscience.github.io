What?

The Helpdesk advises researchers on research-related questions arising at any stage of a project including:

  • Scoping review or systematic review of the literature
  • Study proposal writing
  • Data management plan
  • Study design
  • AVG compliancy
  • Statistical analysis (plan)
  • Data visualizations
  • FAIR data management & data archiving
  • Open science workflows
  • Accurate and complete reporting
  • Open access publishing, pre- and postprints

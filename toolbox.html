Some introduction text about the Open Science Research Manual. Why is it important. What we hope to achieve. Also, we may not cover the wide range and applications of practice-oriented research so the manual will be constantly updated with new insights. However, we think that research integrity and open science is essential and holds true for all research disciplines. The manual is based on Netherlands Code of Conduct for Research Integrity, anti research waste movement and principles of open science and good research practices. Several levels (international, institutional, etc); the handbook is primarily intended for researchers, research teams at the level of the research process. Focus is on scientific integrity and open science. Of course there are other forms of integrity and quality processes, but this falls outside the scope of this manual https://journals.plos.org/plosmedicine/article?id=10.1371/journal.pmed.1001747

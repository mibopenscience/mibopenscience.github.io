How to get started doing open science?

Get an ORCID

Preregister your trial or study at OSF or ...

Create a timely Data Management Plan and Statistical Analysis Plan

Share your code or syntax in Github

Publish open access via the golden or green route

Use reporting guidelines

Report all protocol-stipulated outcomes or account for any changes

Acknodledge all important limitations

Deposit your research data as FAIR as possible in UvA/HvA Figshare with a DOI and license
